# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep communication respectful, constructive, and focused on
technical outcomes.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional and specific in feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Keep review discussions evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may
limit participation for repeated violations.
